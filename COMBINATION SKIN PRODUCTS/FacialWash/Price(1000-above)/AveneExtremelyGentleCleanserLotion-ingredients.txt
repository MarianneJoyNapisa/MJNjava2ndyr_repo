1. Avène Thermal Spring Water
     > Derived from the Avène thermal spring, known for its soothing properties, helps calm and soften the skin.

2. Cetearyl Alcohol
     > A fatty alcohol that acts as an emollient, providing a smooth and soft texture to the skin.

3. Serine
     > An amino acid that helps maintain the skin's natural moisture balance.

4. Cetrimonium Bromide
     > A quaternary ammonium compound with antimicrobial properties, commonly used in hair and skincare products.

5. Coco-Glucoside
     > A gentle surfactant derived from coconut oil and glucose, contributes to the product's cleansing ability.

6. Disodium EDTA
     > A chelating agent that helps stabilize the formula by binding to metal ions, preventing product degradation.

7. Piroctone Olamine
     > Antifungal agent, often used in skincare products to address dandruff or other fungal-related skin concerns.

8. Sodium Benzoate
     > A preservative that prevents the growth of bacteria and fungi in the product.

9. Sodium Cetearyl Sulfate
     > An emulsifying agent that helps combine water and oil-based ingredients in the product.

10. Water
     > The base ingredient, providing a liquid medium for other components.
