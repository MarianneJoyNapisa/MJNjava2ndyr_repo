16 INGREDIENTS:

Water, Glycerin, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Acrylates Copolymer, Sodium Methyl Cocoyl Taurate, Sodium Chloride, Disodium Cocoamphodiacetate, Phenoxyethanol, Citric Acid, Potassium Hydroxide, Hexylene Glycol, Squalane, Allantoin, Butylene Glycol, Centella Asiatica Extract, Disodium Edta

1. Water
     > The base of the product, providing a liquid foundation for other ingredients.

2. Glycerin
     > A humectant that attracts and retains moisture, helping to hydrate the skin.

3. Cocamidopropyl Betaine
     > A mild surfactant that enhances foaming and cleansing properties.

4. Acrylates Copolymer
     > A film-forming agent that contributes to the product's texture and stability.

5. Sodium Methyl Cocoyl Taurate
     > A gentle surfactant that helps cleanse the skin.

6. Sodium Chloride
     > Commonly known as table salt, it may contribute to product viscosity and texture.

7. Disodium Cocoamphodiacetate
     > Another mild surfactant that aids in cleansing.

8. Phenoxyethanol
     > A preservative used to prevent microbial growth in the product.

9. Citric Acid
     > Helps adjust the pH of the product.

10. Potassium Hydroxide
     > A pH adjuster used to control the acidity or alkalinity of the formulation.

11.Hexylene Glycol
     > A solvent and skin-conditioning agent that helps improve product texture.

12. Squalane
     > A moisturizing ingredient that helps soften and smooth the skin.

13. Allantoin
     > Known for its soothing properties, promoting skin healing and regeneration.

14. Butylene Glycol
     > A humectant that helps enhance product texture and skin hydration.

15. Centella Asiatica Extract
     > Contains antioxidants and is known for its skin-soothing properties.

16. Disodium EDTA
     > A chelating agent that helps improve the stability of the formulation.



BENEFICIAL INGREDIENTS:

1. Glycerin, Squalane, Allantoin
     > Provide hydration and soothing benefits for the skin.

2. Centella Asiatica Extract
     > Known for its calming and antioxidant properties.



DEBATABLE INGREDIENTS:

1. Phenoxyethanol
     > Some individuals may be sensitive to preservatives, so patch testing is recommended.

2. Citric Acid and Potassium Hydroxide
     > Adjust the pH of the product; however, high concentrations may be harsh for sensitive skin.
