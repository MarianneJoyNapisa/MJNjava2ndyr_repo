12. Behentrimonium Methosulfate

13. Sodium Lauroyl Lactylate
     > An emulsifier and stabilizer that contributes to the product's texture.

14. Sodium Hyaluronate

15. Cholesterol
     > Supports the skin barrier, enhancing moisture retention.

16. Phenoxyethanol
     > A preservative that prevents bacterial growth in the product.

17. Disodium EDTA

18. Tocopherol

19. Phytosphingosine
     > Lipid with anti-inflammatory properties, supporting the skin barrier.

20. Xanthan Gum
     > A thickening agent that helps create a desirable product texture.

21. Ethylhexylglycerin
     > A preservative and skin-conditioning agent.



BENEFICIAL INGREDIENTS:

1. Glycerin, Sodium Hyaluronate, Cholesterol
     > Hydrate and support the skin barrier.

2. Ceramides
     > Enhance the skin's natural protective layer.



DEBATABLE INGREDIENTS:

1. Petrolatum
     > While effective, some individuals prefer alternatives due to its heavy feel.

2. Phenoxyethanol and Disodium EDTA
     > Some may have sensitivities, but they are generally considered safe in low concentrations.
