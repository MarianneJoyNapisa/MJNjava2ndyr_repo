3. Glycerin: Attracts and retains moisture, helping to hydrate the skin.

4. Propanediol: A skin-conditioning agent that enhances the spreadability of the product.

5. Pentylene Glycol: A humectant and preservative that helps keep the product stable and moisturizes the skin.

6. Niacinamide: Also known as Vitamin B3, it has various skincare benefits, including improving the appearance of enlarged pores, uneven skin tone, and fine lines.

7. Ammonium Polyacryloyldimethyl Taurate: A thickening agent and stabilizer that contributes to the product's texture.

8. Caprylyl Glycol: Acts as a skin-conditioning agent and preservative, enhancing the product's texture and shelf life.

9. Citric Acid: A weak organic acid used to adjust the product's pH.

10. Xanthan Gum: A thickening and stabilizing agent that helps create a consistent texture.
