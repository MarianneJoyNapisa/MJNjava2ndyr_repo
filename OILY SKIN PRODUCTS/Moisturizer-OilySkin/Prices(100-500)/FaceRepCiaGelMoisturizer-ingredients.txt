9. Cetearyl Olivate, Sorbitan Olivate: Emulsifiers that help blend water and oil-based ingredients.

10. Caprylhydroxamic Acid: A chelating agent that helps improve the stability of the formula.

11. Allantoin: Known for its soothing and skin-conditioning properties.

12. Betaine: Functions as a humectant and helps maintain skin hydration.

13. Panthenol: Also known as Provitamin B5, it helps to moisturize and improve skin texture.

BENEFICIAL INGREDIENTS:

1. Centella Asiatica Extract: Soothing and anti-inflammatory.

2. Glycerin, Butylene Glycol, Betaine: Provide hydration to the skin.

3. Allantoin, Panthenol: Soothing and skin-conditioning.



DEBATABLE INGREDIENTS:

1. Essential Oils (Tea Tree and Eucalyptus): While they may have beneficial properties, essential oils can be sensitizing for some individuals, and it's essential to patch-test, especially for those with sensitive skin.
