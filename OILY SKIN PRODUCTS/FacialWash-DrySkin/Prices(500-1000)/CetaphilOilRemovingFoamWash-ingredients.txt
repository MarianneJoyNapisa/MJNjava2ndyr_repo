DEBATABLE INGREDIENTS:

1. Zinc Coceth Sulfate

2. PEG-75, PEG-200 Hydrogenated Glyceryl Palmate, PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil, PEG-7 Glyceryl Cocoate
     > Some individuals may be sensitive to certain PEG compounds, and there are concerns about potential contamination during the manufacturing process.

     > Fragrances can cause skin irritation in some individuals, especially those with sensitive skin. Fragrance-free options may be preferable for some.
