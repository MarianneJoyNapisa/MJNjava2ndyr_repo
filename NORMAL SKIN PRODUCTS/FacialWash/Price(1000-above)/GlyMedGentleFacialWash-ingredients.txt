19 INGREDIENTS:

Ammonium Lauryl Sulfate, Aqua (Water), Glycolic Acid, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Glycerin, Glycol Stearate, Sodium Hydroxide, Diazolidinyl Urea, Citric Acid


1. Ammonium Lauryl Sulfate
     > A surfactant that helps cleanse the skin by breaking down oils and dirt.

2. Water
     > The base of the formulation, providing a liquid medium for other ingredients.

3. Glycolic Acid
     > An alpha hydroxy acid (AHA) that exfoliates the skin, promoting cell turnover and addressing issues like dullness and fine lines.

4. Cocamidopropyl Betaine
     > A mild surfactant derived from coconut oil, contributing to the product's foaming properties.

5. Glycerin
     > A humectant that attracts and retains moisture, helping to keep the skin hydrated.

6. Glycol Stearate
     > A conditioning agent and emollient that helps improve the texture of the product.

7. Sodium Hydroxide
     > Also known as lye, it is used to adjust the pH of the formulation.

8. Diazolidinyl Urea
     > A preservative that helps prevent the growth of microorganisms in the product.

9. Citric Acid
     > Used to adjust the pH of the formulation and may also contribute to exfoliation.



BENEFICIAL INGREDIENTS:

1. Glycolic Acid
     > Exfoliates the skin, promoting a smoother and brighter complexion.

2. Glycerin
     > Hydrates and moisturizes the skin.

3. Cocamidopropyl Betaine
     > A mild cleanser that contributes to the product's foaming properties.



DEBATABLE INGREDIENTS:

1. Ammonium Lauryl Sulfate
     > Some individuals may find sulfates to be harsh, especially if they have sensitive or dry skin.

2. Diazolidinyl Urea
     > A formaldehyde-releasing preservative, and some people may prefer products without formaldehyde-releasing agents.
